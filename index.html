A Decade of Trusted Service

Started as a PC repair shop. Grown into East Rand's full-service tech company. The same honest service, just a lot more of it.

Our story
From a repair bench
to a full tech company

Mainline Technology was founded over ten years ago by Johan de Bruin as a PC repair business serving the Brakpan community. What started on a workbench in Brakpan grew into a trusted name across the East Rand — built on honest pricing, fast turnaround, and genuine care for every client.

Today, Mainline Technology is run by Joshua de Bruin and his father Johan — carrying the legacy forward with expanded services and new energy. Joshua brings modern web development, app building, and digital expertise; Johan brings the decade of hands-on hardware and community trust.

Together, they've built something rare: a single company where you can get your PC fixed, your website designed, your social media managed, and your IT supported — all with the same honest approach and personal service the community has come to rely on.

Our journey
Founded

Johan starts Mainline Technology

A PC repair business is born in Brakpan, serving the local East Rand community with honest hardware repairs and diagnostics.

Growth

Reputation spreads across East Rand

Word-of-mouth grows the business. Virus removal, data recovery, and IT support are added as demand from homes and small businesses grows.

Expansion

Joshua joins, digital services launch

Joshua brings web development, app building, social media management, and content writing — transforming Mainline into a full-service tech company.

Today

One company for every tech need

Serving hundreds of clients across the East Rand. Same honest values, more services, and a commitment to keeping every client's tech working perfectly.
